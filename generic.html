Information

Ever though it was hard to find the right emoji, comment, hashtag, caption, or filter for your social media. Legend wants to simply that for you.

Legend's primary purpose is to simplify social media for you. All you have to do is take pictures like you normally do and upload them onto Legend. Our advanced machine learning and computer vision algorithms are designed to calculate the mood of your pictures to find the perfect description to go along with your photos. Keep in mind that Legend was built in a 24-hour hackathon. It's still learning everyday to provide more coherent results.

Legend only wants to give you suggestions for social media ease and improvement. In no way do we promote actions of discrimination. You still hold full responsibility to what goes on your social media accounts.
