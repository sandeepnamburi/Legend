What is Legend?

Legend is an artificially intelligent software that takes your social media pics to generate intelligent yet relative hashtags, comments, captions, emojis, and filters for most social media sites and apps. Legend is meant to be used in par with your existing social media accounts and photos. No sign-up. No credit card. 100% free forever.
